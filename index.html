HOW TO PLAY
🏀 DRAFT 8 PLAYERS — snake order, everyone picks one at a time. Order is randomized at league creation.
📊 EARN POINTS — your players score fantasy points each game based on their real stats. Originally drafted players earn escalating round bonuses: +5 R1 · +10 R2 · +20 R3 · +50 Finals (max +85 per player).
🔄 WAIVERS — a slot opens when your player's team is eliminated or you mark a player as OUT. That player goes to the waiver pool and is available for everyone to claim. Claims are processed ASAP by the commissioner to keep things moving — a team could be eliminated mid-day and you may want to grab someone for that night.
⚠️ WAIVER BONUS RULE — players picked up off waivers (at any point, for any reason) earn only a flat +5 bonus per round survived from that point forward — regardless of how far they advance. This applies even if picked up mid-round.
🥇 WIN — most total fantasy points at the end of the NBA Finals takes the crown.
